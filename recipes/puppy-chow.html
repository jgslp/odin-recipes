Puppy Chow

Description

Addictive and unhealthy"

    Ingredients
  • 1 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 9 cups crispy rice cereal squares
  • 1 and 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
      • Steps
  • Melt chocolate in a saucepan over low heat, stirring until smooth
  • Stir in peanut butter and mix until smooth
  • Remove from heat and add cereal; stir until coated
  • Pour powdered sugar into large resealable plastic bag
  • Add cereal and shake until well coated
  • Enjoy
