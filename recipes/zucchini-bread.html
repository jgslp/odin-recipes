Zucchini Bread

Description

The best zucchini bread you'll ever eat.

    Ingredients
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 tablespoon ground cinnamon
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup vegetable oil
  • 2 and 1/4 cups white sugar
  • 3 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 2 cups grated zucchini
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts
      • Steps
  • Preheat oven to 325 degrees Fahrenheit
  • Grease and flour two 8 x 4 inch pans
  • Stir flour, salt, baking powder, soda, and cinnamon together in a bowl
  • Beat eggs, oil, vanilla, and sugar together in a large bowl
  • Add sifted ingredients into the creamed mixture and beat well
  • Stir in zucchini and nuts until well combined
  • Pour batter into prepared pans
  • Bake 40 to 60 minutes, or until tester inserted into the center comes out clean
  • Let cool and enjoy
